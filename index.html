FAQ

The questions you're actually asking

Straight answers, including the compliance ones.

Do I need a separate dialer service or special equipment?+

No. Two ways to use it: dial straight from the built-in browser dialer, or — zero setup — tap record and make the call on the phone already in your hand, on speaker near your computer. Either way the AI hears the call and logs it. No hardware, no Mojo or PhoneBurner subscription on top.

Is it legal to record and transcribe my calls?+

Recording laws vary by state — many states need only one party's consent (yours), but some, including Florida and California, require everyone on the call to consent. The simple fix is the professional one: open with “I'm on a recorded line.” You're responsible for compliance in your state, but a one-line disclosure typically covers it — and it sounds more professional, not less.

What about Do-Not-Call rules when I'm cold calling?+

PitchLog ships with four layers: a personal Do-Not-Call list that blocks numbers everywhere, Compliance Mode enforcing legal calling hours (8am–9pm) in each prospect's time zone, optional registry scrubbing (federal/state DNC + known TCPA litigators) on imported lists, and Cell-Phone Safety Mode that keeps every Power Dialer call human-initiated. The nuance most tools skip: calling an agent's published business landline about business is generally DNC-exempt, but autodialing or AI-voice calls to cell phones can carry TCPA liability of $500–$1,500 per call — which is why PitchLog keeps dialing manually initiated instead of marketing itself as an autodialer.

How accurate are the AI notes?+

The transcript is captured live during the call, then AI extracts the details — names, numbers, loan amounts, outcomes — from what was actually said. You review the card before it matters; edits take seconds. It is dramatically more accurate than what anyone remembers at the end of a 40-call day.

Can I get my data into my real CRM?+

One click exports your whole pipeline as a clean Excel file that imports into Salesforce, HubSpot, Encompass, Jungo — anything. Your data is never held hostage: export everything, even on the free plan, even after you cancel.

What happens when I hit 25 calls on the free plan?+

You get a prompt to upgrade, and that's it. Nothing is deleted — you can still view, search, and export every previous call. The counter resets on the 1st of each month — or any paid plan (Solo from $39/mo annual) removes the cap entirely.

I'm not a mortgage LO — can I still use it?+

Yes. The pricing engine and PDF quotes are mortgage-specific, but the dialer, AI notes, lead scoring, follow-ups, and compliance tools work for any phone-heavy sales role — insurance, real estate, recruiting, B2B.

Can I cancel Pro anytime?+

Anytime, from settings, no questions and no fees. You keep Pro until the end of the billing period and your data stays yours — export it all whenever you like.
