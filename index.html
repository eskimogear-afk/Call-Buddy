FAQ

Common questions answered

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need any special equipment?+

No. All you need is a computer with a built-in microphone and your regular phone. Put your phone on speaker near your computer when making calls and PitchLog picks up both sides of the conversation automatically.

Is the transcription accurate?+

Yes. PitchLog uses your browser built-in speech recognition powered by the same technology as Google Voice Search. Accuracy is very high in normal conditions. Audio quality improves when your phone is placed close to your computer microphone.

Is my call data private and secure?+

Absolutely. Your call data is stored in your personal account with row-level security. No other user can ever access your records. We do not sell or share your data with anyone. You can delete all your data permanently at any time from your account settings.

Can I export my data to my CRM?+

Yes. Every call log can be exported as a formatted Excel file with one click. Most CRMs including Salesforce, HubSpot, and Encompass accept Excel imports, so you can bring your PitchLog data into any system you use.

What happens when I reach 25 calls on the free plan?+

You will see a prompt to upgrade to Pro when you reach your monthly limit. Your existing call data is never deleted. You can still view, search, and export all your previous calls. The limit resets on the 1st of every month.

Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?+

Yes. You can cancel Pro at any time from your account settings. You will keep Pro access until the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees and no questions asked.
