Best Cold Call Scripts for Mortgage Loan Officers

The best cold call scripts for loan officers don't sound like scripts. They earn 15 seconds of attention, ask one sharp question, and book a specific next step.

Script 1: The rate-watcher opener

"Hi [Name], this is [You] with [Company]. I'm reaching out to homeowners in [Area] because rates shifted this week — are you still considering a refinance, or has that moved off your radar?"

Script 2: The referral follow-up

"[Referrer] mentioned you might be looking at options before [season/event]. Do you have 2 minutes, or is there a better time tomorrow?"

Script 3: The appointment close

"Based on what you shared, a 15-minute call would tell us if it's worth running numbers. I have Tuesday at 10 or Wednesday at 2 — which works?"
