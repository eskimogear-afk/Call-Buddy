Program description

Program name: Visto Mortgage — Damon Cooper Mortgage Updates. This program sends one-to-one text messages from Damon Cooper, a licensed mortgage loan officer operating as Visto Mortgage, including follow-ups to phone conversations, appointment scheduling, missed-call notifications, and replies to questions you send us.

Opt-in

You will only receive messages if you provided your phone number directly to us and agreed to receive texts (for example, during a phone conversation with Damon), or if you initiated contact by calling or texting our business number first.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on your conversation with us — typically a few messages per month. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Opt-out (STOP)

You can cancel at any time. Text STOP to unsubscribe. After you send STOP, we will confirm your unsubscribe and you will receive no further messages from us. To rejoin, text START or let us know by phone.

Help (HELP)
